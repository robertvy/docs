Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a minute?

We charge per minute of conversation time. This includes the time your AI teammate is speaking and listening. We bill in 1-second increments.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect immediately.

Is there a setup fee?

No. All standard plans have zero setup fees. For Enterprise plans requiring custom integration or on-premise deployment, we'll quote a one-time implementation fee.

What happens if I go over my minutes?

Your service continues uninterrupted! We'll simply charge the overage rate (visible in the pricing table) for additional minutes used.
