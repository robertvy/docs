## ✍️ Brand Voice

### How We Sound
**Professional, Clear, Confident, Precise, Partnership-Oriented**

✅ **Good:**
"Automate 70% of routine calls while maintaining GDPR compliance. Production-ready in 5 days."

❌ **Bad:**
"Revolutionize your customer experience with our groundbreaking AI technology!"

### Key Principles
1. **Specifics over superlatives** - Show metrics, not hype
2. **Active voice** - Clear subjects and actions
3. **Proof-based** - Every claim backed by data
4. **German precision** - Thorough, accurate, complete
5. **One idea per sentence** - Clarity over density

## 🔑 Brand Dos and Don'ts

### ✅ DO:
- Use specific metrics ("70% automation rate")
- Emphasize European data sovereignty
- Show compliance documentation
- Write in active voice with clear subjects
- Provide proof points (case studies, metrics)

### ❌ DON'T:
- Use hype words ("revolutionary," "disruptive")
- Make vague claims without proof
- Use generic stock photography
- Ignore German language considerations
- Compete on features alone (emphasize trust)

## 🆘 Brand Questions?

### Before Publishing Anything, Ask:

1. **Is it true?** Can we prove this claim?
2. **Is it clear?** Would a non-expert understand?
3. **Is it specific?** Did we use concrete numbers/examples?
4. **Is it relevant?** Does this matter to our audience?
5. **Is it differentiated?** Could a competitor say the same?
6. **Is it on-brand?** Does it sound like us?

**If "No" to Any:** Review the relevant section in the brand guides.
