# Solution Partner Agreement

# Part I: Foundation and Partner Relationship

## 1. Preamble and Relationship to Terms and Conditions

**1.1. Governing Documents:** This Solution Partner Agreement ("Agreement") supplements and operates in conjunction with itellico's General Terms and Conditions ("GTC") available at [Terms and Conditions](/legal/terms-and-conditions). All provisions of the GTC apply to Partners unless specifically modified or supplemented by this Agreement. In case of conflict between this Agreement and the GTC, this Agreement takes precedence for partner-specific matters.

**1.2. Partner Prerequisites:** To become and remain a Solution Partner, the Partner must:
- a) Execute this Agreement with itellico;
- b) Maintain an active itellico AI Package subscription as specified in Section 4.3;
- c) Agree to and comply with the GTC in their own capacity as a User; and
- d) Complete initial partner certification as outlined in Section 3.

**1.3. Scope of Partnership:** This Agreement governs the Partner's right to acquire itellico Services for resale to End Customers as part of integrated solutions, subject to the terms herein and the underlying GTC.

## 2. Partner-Specific Definitions

**2.1. Additional Definitions:** In addition to definitions in Section 2 of the GTC, the following terms apply specifically to this Agreement:

- **Solution Partner (Partner):** The independent contractor who acquires Services from itellico under this Agreement and resells them in their own name and account to End Customers, typically as part of bundled solutions with their own services.
- **End Customer:** The ultimate user of itellico Services who acquires them from the Partner as part of a solution, distinct from the Partner's direct relationship with itellico.
- **AI Package:** The Partner's chosen itellico subscription plan (e.g., Starter, Growth, Business, Scale) with defined service scope and pricing per the public price list at [Pricing](/pricing).
- **Partner Portal:** The dedicated online platform provided by itellico for partner management, resources, and administration, accessible at [https://partner.itellico.ai](https://partner.itellico.ai).
- **Partner Tier:** Classification levels (Registered Partner (Certified), Silver Partner, Gold Partner, Platinum Partner) determining access to enhanced benefits and privileges.
- **White Labeling:** The right to offer itellico Services under the Partner's own branding, available to Gold and Platinum Partners.
- **Lead Registration:** The voluntary system for Partners to register potential customers to secure exclusive processing rights for defined periods.
- **Enterprise Rates:** Individually negotiated pricing for Enterprise solutions, agreed with the Partner Account Manager and displayed in the Partner Portal.
- **Market Development Funds (MDF):** Financial support provided to qualifying Partners for approved marketing and sales activities.

**2.2. GTC Definitions Apply:** All other definitions from Section 2 of the GTC apply to this Agreement unless specifically redefined above.

## 3. Partner Training and Certification

**3.1. Initial Certification:** Partners must successfully complete itellico's basic certification webinar to achieve **Registered Partner (Certified)** status. Details and scheduling are available in the Partner Portal.

**3.2. Advanced Certifications:** itellico may offer optional advanced certifications to demonstrate specialized expertise. While not mandatory for higher Partner Tiers, they may contribute to tier advancement criteria as defined in the Partner Portal.

**3.3. Training Resources:** itellico provides training materials, documentation, and support resources through the Partner Portal to ensure Partners can effectively represent and implement itellico Services.

# Part II: Partner Rights and Obligations

## 4. Partner Role and Service Acquisition

**4.1. Independent Contractor Status:** The Partner operates as an independent contractor, not as an agent or employee of itellico. The Partner has no authority to legally bind itellico or create obligations on itellico's behalf.

**4.2. Service Acquisition Rights:** itellico grants the Partner a non-exclusive right to:
- a) Acquire itellico Services at standard conditions per the public price list at [Pricing](/pricing);
- b) Resell these Services in the Partner's own name and account to End Customers;
- c) Integrate itellico Services into comprehensive solutions with added value through consulting, implementation, and support.

**4.3. Required Partner Subscription:** Partners must maintain an active itellico AI Package at minimum "Growth" level (or equivalent as defined by itellico). This subscription must be:
- a) Ordered separately from itellico;
- b) Activated within 7 days of this Agreement's effective date;
- c) Maintained throughout the partnership duration.

**4.4. End Customer Provisioning:** Partners provision itellico Services for End Customers through sub-accounts linked to their Partner account. This provisioning right is exclusive to Solution Partners under this Agreement.

## 5. Lead Registration and Channel Management

**5.1. Lead Registration System:** itellico may provide a lead registration system to prevent channel conflicts and protect Partner sales efforts. Registration procedures and benefits are detailed at [Lead Registration Guide](/leads).

**5.2. Registration Benefits:** Successful lead registration may provide:
- a) Exclusive processing rights for the registered lead;
- b) Protection period as defined in the Lead Registration Guide;
- c) Clear opportunity tracking and management.

**5.3. Non-Exclusive Rights:** itellico retains the right to sell Services directly to customers and appoint other distribution partners. The lead registration system provides coordination mechanisms when utilized by Partners.

## 6. Partner Tiers and Benefits

**6.1. Tier Structure:** The partner program includes four tiers with ascending benefits:

| Partner Tier | Key Benefits |
|--------------|-------------|
| **Registered Partner (Certified)** | Basic certification, Partner Portal access, standard marketing materials, basic support, lead registration |
| **Silver Partner** | All Registered Partner benefits plus public directory listing, co-branding materials, potential MDF eligibility |
| **Gold Partner** | All Silver Partner benefits plus White Labeling rights, dedicated Partner Account Manager, prioritized support, enhanced MDF eligibility, beta program access |
| **Platinum Partner** | All Gold Partner benefits plus strategic advisory participation, maximum MDF eligibility, dedicated technical resources, custom integration support |

**6.2. Qualification Criteria:** Detailed criteria for achieving and maintaining each tier (including usage volume, revenue, certifications, customer success metrics) are defined in the Partner Portal and by itellico management, and reviewed annually.

**6.3. White Labeling Rights:** Gold and Platinum Partners may offer itellico Services under their own branding within technical capabilities and guidelines provided in the Partner Portal. Partners remain responsible for trademark and competition law compliance.

# Part III: Commercial Terms and Pricing

## 7. Pricing and Payment Structure

**7.1. Standard Pricing Application:** Partners acquire Services at standard conditions per the public price list at [Pricing](/pricing), except where Enterprise Rates apply per Section 7.2.

**7.2. Enterprise Rates:** Partners with explicit Enterprise status or special Enterprise packages may negotiate individual pricing with their Partner Account Manager. Agreed rates are displayed in the Partner Portal.

**7.3. Usage-Based Billing:**
- a) Partner's AI Package includes usage quotas for aggregated consumption (Partner use plus all End Customer sub-accounts);
- b) Usage exceeding included quotas incurs charges at standard overage rates;
- c) Monthly billing includes package fees and any overage charges.

**7.4. Partner Margin Model:** While Partners set their own pricing to End Customers for the complete solution, Partner margins derive from pricing their value-added services (consulting, implementation, support, solution integration) rather than from marking up itellico Services costs. Partners pay itellico's standard rates and create value through their professional services and solution delivery.

**7.5. Professional Services:** itellico Professional Services (consulting, development, enhanced support) are available at standard hourly rates per [Billing Rates](/billing-rates). Potential discounts may be individually negotiated with the Partner Account Manager.

**7.6. Payment Terms:** Standard payment terms from Section 12 of the GTC apply, including payment due upon invoice receipt, late payment consequences, and tax responsibilities.

## 8. Market Development Funds and Support

**8.1. MDF Eligibility:** Silver Partners and above may qualify for Market Development Funds to support approved marketing and sales activities for itellico-based solutions.

**8.2. MDF Application:** MDF requests require prior approval per guidelines in the Partner Portal. Funds support activities that promote itellico Services within Partner solutions.

**8.3. Support Levels:** Partner support escalation paths and service levels correspond to achieved Partner Tier, with enhanced support for higher tiers including dedicated account management and technical resources.

# Part IV: End Customer Management

## 9. End Customer Relationships and Compliance

**9.1. Partner Responsibility:** Partners are solely responsible for:
- a) Contracting with End Customers for solution delivery;
- b) Setting End Customer pricing and terms;
- c) Billing and payment collection from End Customers;
- d) First-level support for the complete solution provided.

**9.2. GTC Compliance for End Customers:** Partners must ensure End Customers either:
- a) Directly agree to itellico's GTC at [Terms and Conditions](/legal/terms-and-conditions) and Privacy Policy at [Privacy Policy](/legal/privacy-policy); or
- b) Are bound by Partner's own terms providing comparable protection and covering essential aspects of itellico's documents.

**9.3. Required End Customer Information:** Partners must collect and provide to itellico the name and Administrator Email account (as defined in the GTC) of each End Customer's responsible administrator for:
- a) Operational service provision;
- b) GDPR compliance requirements;
- c) Limited direct communication as specified in Section 9.4.

**9.4. Direct End Customer Communication:** itellico may use End Customer administrator email addresses exclusively for:
- a) Security-relevant information regarding Services;
- b) Significant regulatory change notifications;
- c) Partner & Security Newsletter distribution.
General marketing communications require separate End Customer consent.

**9.5. End Customer Compliance Enforcement:** Partners must include provisions in End Customer contracts ensuring compliance with itellico's Acceptable Use Policy per Section 7.2 of the GTC. Partners are liable for End Customer violations as if for their own conduct.

# Part V: Intellectual Property and Data Rights

## 10. Partner-Specific Intellectual Property Provisions

**10.1. GTC IP Rights Apply:** All intellectual property provisions from Section 9 of the GTC apply to Partners, including itellico's retention of platform rights and Partner ownership of original Inputs.

**10.2. Partner Input Rights:** Partners retain intellectual property rights in specific prompts and configurations ("Partner Inputs") created for End Customer implementations, subject to:
- a) itellico's license for service improvement per Section 9.3 of the GTC;
- b) Partner's obligation to license Partner Inputs to End Customers for service use.

**10.3. End Customer Licensing:** Partners must ensure End Customers receive non-exclusive, non-transferable licenses to use Partner Inputs in connection with itellico Services for the duration of the Partner's service provision.

**10.4. Anonymized Data Usage:** Partners grant itellico rights to use all data from Partner and End Customer accounts in strictly anonymized and aggregated form for service improvement, development, and AI model training, as detailed in Section 9.3 of the GTC.

# Part VI: Agreement Management

## 11. Term, Modification, and Termination

**11.1. Agreement Term:** This Agreement continues indefinitely until terminated by either party with 3 months' written notice to the end of a calendar quarter, consistent with Section 17.1 of the GTC.

**11.2. Termination for Cause:** Either party may terminate immediately for material breach, insolvency, or other causes specified in Section 17.2 of the GTC. itellico may also terminate for Partner violations of Section 6.17 (legal compliance) or Section 6.18 (anti-corruption) without cure period.

**11.3. Change of Control:** Parties must notify each other within 30 days of any change of control (>50% ownership change, merger). The other party may terminate with immediate effect within 30 days of notification.

**11.4. Consequences of Termination:** Upon termination:
- a) Partner rights to acquire and market Services cease immediately;
- b) Partner must settle all outstanding invoices;
- c) Existing End Customer relationships remain Partner's responsibility;
- d) Continued itellico Service provision for End Customers requires separate arrangements.

## 12. Data Portability and Transition Support

**12.1. End Customer Data Rights:** Partners must not prevent End Customers from exercising data export rights under itellico's GTC. Partners should assist End Customers in accessing itellico's direct data export functionality.

**12.2. Transition Support:** Partners must provide reasonable support to ensure End Customers understand and can utilize data export capabilities, particularly upon relationship termination.

# Part VII: Legal Framework

## 13. Governing Provisions

**13.1. GTC Integration:** The following sections of the GTC apply directly to this Agreement:
- a) Section 10 (Data Protection & Processing) - including DPA requirements;
- b) Section 11 (Confidentiality Obligations);
- c) Section 15 (Limitation of Liability & Indemnification);
- d) Section 19 (Governing Law & Dispute Resolution);
- e) Section 20 (Final Clauses) - including force majeure, notices, and survival provisions.

**13.2. Partner-Specific Indemnification:** In addition to GTC indemnification provisions, Partners specifically indemnify itellico against claims arising from:
- a) Partner's End Customer relationships and representations;
- b) Partner's compliance failures under this Agreement;
- c) Partner's brand features or their use by itellico under this Agreement.

**13.3. Amendment Process:** This Agreement may be amended only in writing. Changes to Partner Portal guidelines and processes may be communicated electronically with reasonable notice.

**13.4. Survival:** Upon termination, the following provisions survive: Sections 1 (Foundation), 9.4 (Communication Understanding), 10 (IP Provisions), 13 (Legal Framework), and all payment obligations, plus applicable GTC survival provisions.

**13.5. Jurisdiction and Law:** Austrian law governs this Agreement, with exclusive jurisdiction in Vienna, consistent with Section 19 of the GTC.
