## Changing Plans

### Upgrading

When you upgrade:
- New features available immediately
- Included minutes prorated for remaining billing period
- No interruption to service

### Downgrading

When you downgrade:
- Takes effect at next billing cycle
- Excess resources (agents, knowledge bases) may become read-only
- No refunds for unused time

<Warning>
Before downgrading, ensure your usage fits within the new plan's limits. Resources exceeding limits won't be deleted but may become inaccessible until you upgrade or remove them.
</Warning>

## Annual vs Monthly

| Billing | Discount | Commitment |
|---------|----------|------------|
| Monthly | None | Cancel anytime |
| Annual | 17% off | 12-month commitment |

Annual plans are paid upfront. If you cancel mid-year, you won't receive a refund for remaining months, but service continues until the end of your paid period.
