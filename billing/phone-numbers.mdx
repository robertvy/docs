## Managing Phone Numbers

### Getting Numbers

1. Go to **Phone Numbers** in your dashboard
2. Click **Get Number**
3. Select country and number type
4. Choose from available numbers
5. Assign to an agent

Numbers are available immediately after purchase.

### Releasing Numbers

1. Go to **Phone Numbers**
2. Select the number to release
3. Click **Release Number**
4. Confirm release

<Warning>
Released numbers cannot be recovered. Ensure you've updated any published materials before releasing a number.
</Warning>

### Number Porting

Bring your existing numbers to itellicoAI:

1. Contact support with your porting request
2. Provide current carrier information
3. Sign Letter of Authorization (LOA)
4. Wait for port completion (typically 2-4 weeks)

Porting fees vary by country and carrier.

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Use local numbers for local presence" icon="location-dot">
    Calls from local numbers have higher answer rates. Use numbers that match your target audience's region.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Consider toll-free for inbound support" icon="phone-volume">
    Toll-free numbers encourage customers to call since they won't be charged. Great for support lines.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Release unused numbers" icon="trash">
    Don't pay for numbers you're not using. Review and release inactive numbers monthly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Plan for international expansion" icon="globe">
    If expanding internationally, get numbers in target markets before launch to ensure availability.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
