## Overview

Our Partner Program is designed for agencies and developers who build end-to-end voice AI solutions for clients. Gain access to exclusive resources, qualified leads, and strategic support to grow your practice.

## Partner Tiers

Progress through three tier levels based on customer onboarding and content creation.

### Silver Level

**Requirements**
- Onboard 1-5 customers in 6 months
- Create 1-3 technical guides or videos
- Complete partner certification

**Benefits**
- Access to qualified leads
- Partner badge and directory listing
- Collaborative marketing opportunities
- Partner support channel
- Exclusive partner training

### Gold Level

**Requirements**
- Onboard 6-12 customers in 6 months
- Create 4-8 technical blogs or videos
- Maintain positive customer feedback

**Benefits**
- All Silver benefits, plus:
- Dedicated partner webinar
- Create and resell custom templates
- Early access to beta features
- Co-marketing campaign opportunities
- Higher lead priority

### Diamond Level

**Requirements**
- Onboard 13+ customers in 6 months
- Create 9+ technical resources
- Active community contribution

**Benefits**
- All Gold benefits, plus:
- Priority support for all projects
- Weekly strategic meetings with founding team
- Revenue sharing on template sales
- Featured partner status
- Exclusive Diamond partner events

## Tier Advancement

- Tier status maintained for minimum 6 months
- Evaluated semi-annually based on performance
- Clear path to advancement with measurable metrics

## How to Join

### 1. Submit Application
Complete our [partner application form](#) with:
- Company information
- Technical expertise
- Target market and use cases
- Previous voice AI projects (if any)

### 2. Initial Review
Our team will review your application based on:
- Technical capabilities
- Market alignment
- Growth potential
- Customer service track record

### 3. Partner Meeting
Approved applicants meet with our partner team to:
- Discuss partnership goals
- Review program details
- Create success plan
- Set initial milestones

### 4. Certification
Complete our partner certification including:
- Platform fundamentals
- Best practices for voice AI
- Customer success strategies
- Technical implementation

### 5. Launch
Once certified, you'll receive:
- Partner portal access
- Marketing materials
- Lead flow activation
- Dedicated partner manager
