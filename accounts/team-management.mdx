## Inviting Team Members

Add new users to your account through email invitations.

### Who Can Invite

- **Owner**: ✅ Can invite anyone
- **Admin**: ✅ Can invite anyone
- **Member**: ❌ Cannot invite
- **Viewer**: ❌ Cannot invite

### How to Invite

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team** in your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite Members">
    Click the **Invite Members** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Email Addresses">
    Add one or more email addresses (comma-separated)

    New members default to **Member** role
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Role (Optional)">
    Change the role dropdown if needed (Admin, Member, or Viewer)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Invitation">
    Click **Send Invitations**

    Invitees receive an email with a secure link
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Invitations expire after 7 days. You can resend expired invitations from the team page.
</Note>

### Invitation Email

Invitees receive an email containing:
- Who invited them
- Account name
- Secure acceptance link
- Expiration date (7 days)

## Managing Invitations

### Viewing Pending Invitations

See all sent invitations that haven't been accepted:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the **Pending Invitations** tab
3. View status of each invitation

### Invitation Statuses

- **Pending**: Awaiting response
- **Accepted**: User joined the team
- **Declined**: User rejected the invitation
- **Expired**: Past the 7-day window
- **Cancelled**: You cancelled before acceptance

### Resending Invitations

For pending or expired invitations:

1. Find the invitation in **Pending Invitations**
2. Click **Resend**
3. A new invitation email is sent with a fresh 7-day expiration

### Cancelling Invitations

To cancel a pending invitation:

1. Find the invitation in **Pending Invitations**
2. Click the menu (⋯) → **Cancel**
3. The invitation link becomes invalid

<Tip>
Cancel invitations if you sent to the wrong email or the person is no longer joining.
</Tip>

## Accepting Invitations

### As an Invitee

When you receive an invitation:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Your Email">
    Look for an email from itellicoAI with the invitation
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the Link">
    Click the **Accept Invitation** button in the email
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign In or Sign Up">
    - **Existing user**: Sign in with your account
    - **New user**: Create an account with the invited email
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Review the account details and click **Join Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Start Working">
    You're now a member! Switch to the account using the account switcher.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Declining Invitations

If you don't want to join:

1. Click the link in the invitation email
2. Click **Decline Invitation**
3. Optionally provide a reason
4. The inviter is notified

## Viewing Team Members

### Team List

The team page shows all members with:
- **Name & Email**
- **Avatar**
- **Role** (Owner, Admin, Member, Viewer)
- **Status** (Active, Pending)
- **Last Login** (when they last accessed the account)
- **Join Date**

### Filtering & Searching

- **Search**: Find members by name or email
- **Filter by Role**: Show only Owners, Admins, Members, or Viewers
- **Filter by Status**: Active members vs pending invitations

## Changing Member Roles

### Who Can Change Roles

- **Owner**: Can change any role (except their own Owner role)
- **Admin**: Can change Member and Viewer roles
- **Member**: Cannot change roles
- **Viewer**: Cannot change roles

### How to Change

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Member">
    Locate the team member in the list
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Role Dropdown">
    Click the dropdown showing their current role
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select New Role">
    Choose Admin, Member, or Viewer

    (Owner cannot be selected - contact support for ownership transfer)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    The role changes immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Role changes are immediate and affect the user's access right away. Make sure you're selecting the correct role!
</Warning>

## Removing Team Members

### Who Can Remove

- **Owner**: Can remove anyone (except themselves)
- **Admin**: Can remove Members and Viewers (not Owner or other Admins)
- **Member**: Cannot remove anyone
- **Viewer**: Cannot remove anyone

### How to Remove

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Member">
    Locate the team member you want to remove
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open Menu">
    Click the menu icon (⋯) next to their name
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Remove">
    Select **Remove from Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Removal">
    Confirm that you want to remove this member

    They will lose access immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Removed members lose all access immediately. They won't be able to view agents, conversations, or any account data.
</Warning>

### What Happens When Removed

- ❌ Access revoked immediately
- ❌ Can no longer see the account in their account switcher
- ❌ Cannot access any data or agents
- ✅ Content they created remains in the account
- ✅ Can be re-invited later if needed

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Onboarding New Members" icon="user-plus">
    **Prepare before inviting:**
    1. Determine the correct role
    2. Prepare welcome documentation
    3. Assign a buddy or mentor
    4. Schedule an orientation call

    **After they join:**
    1. Give them a tour of the platform
    2. Grant access to relevant agents
    3. Share team conventions and standards
    4. Check in after the first week
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Role Assignment Strategy" icon="user-shield">
    **Start conservative:**
    - Default to **Viewer** or **Member**
    - Promote to Admin only when needed
    - Limit Admin role to team leads
    - Reserve Owner for account leadership

    **Regular reviews:**
    - Quarterly access audits
    - Demote when responsibilities change
    - Remove departing employees immediately
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation Management" icon="envelope">
    **Best practices:**
    - Double-check email addresses
    - Include a personal message
    - Set calendar reminder to follow up after 3 days
    - Cancel invitations for bounced emails
    - Resend if no response after 5 days
    - Clean up expired invitations monthly
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Offboarding" icon="user-minus">
    **When someone leaves:**
    1. Remove their access immediately
    2. Document what they were working on
    3. Reassign their agents/projects
    4. Update API keys if they had access
    5. Review and revoke any API keys they created
    6. Update team documentation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="External Collaborators" icon="user-tie">
    **For contractors, consultants, or partners:**
    - Use **Viewer** role initially
    - Promote to **Member** only if creating content
    - Never give Admin/Owner to external parties
    - Set calendar reminder for access review
    - Remove access when engagement ends
    - Consider using dedicated subaccounts for clients
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invitation not received" icon="envelope">
    **Common causes:**
    - Email in spam folder
    - Typo in email address
    - Email blocked by recipient's server

    **Solutions:**
    1. Ask them to check spam
    2. Verify the email address is correct
    3. Resend the invitation
    4. Try an alternative email address
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't remove a member" icon="ban">
    **Common causes:**
    - You're not Owner/Admin
    - Trying to remove the Owner
    - Trying to remove yourself as Owner

    **Solutions:**
    - Only Owners can remove Admins
    - Owner cannot be removed (transfer ownership first)
    - Contact an Owner if you need assistance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation expired" icon="clock">
    **Solution:**
    1. Go to **Settings → Team → Pending Invitations**
    2. Find the expired invitation
    3. Click **Resend**
    4. A new 7-day invitation is sent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="User can't see the account after accepting" icon="eye-slash">
    **Common causes:**
    - User signed in with wrong email
    - Multiple accounts with similar names
    - Account deactivated after invitation

    **Solutions:**
    - Verify they used the correct email
    - Have them check all accounts in switcher
    - Check if account is still active
    - Resend invitation if needed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need to change Owner" icon="crown">
    **This requires support:**
    - Contact support@itellico.ai
    - Provide current and new owner emails
    - Confirm from current owner
    - Support will process the transfer

    **Cannot be done in UI** for security reasons.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Considerations

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verify Email Addresses" icon="shield-check">
    Before inviting:
    - Double-check spelling
    - Verify the domain (watch for typos like .co vs .com)
    - Confirm it's a business email if required
    - Never invite personal emails for company accounts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Departure Checklist" icon="list-check">
    When someone leaves:
    - [ ] Remove from all accounts immediately
    - [ ] Revoke any API keys they created
    - [ ] Change shared passwords they knew
    - [ ] Review their recent activity
    - [ ] Update emergency contacts if they were listed
    - [ ] Transfer ownership if they were Owner
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Access Reviews" icon="calendar-check">
    Schedule quarterly reviews:
    - [ ] List all team members
    - [ ] Verify each person still needs access
    - [ ] Check if roles are still appropriate
    - [ ] Remove inactive users
    - [ ] Document the review date and findings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Suspicious Activity" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    If you notice unusual activity:
    - Review recent logins in team member details
    - Check for unknown team members
    - Verify all pending invitations
    - Remove suspicious accounts immediately
    - Change API keys if needed
    - Contact support if you suspect compromise
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
