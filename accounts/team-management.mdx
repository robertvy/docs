## Inviting Team Members

Add new users to your account by sending email invitations.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team** in your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Invite Members">
    Click the **Invite Members** button
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enter Email Addresses">
    Add one or more email addresses (one per line or comma-separated)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send Invitations">
    Click **Send Invitations**

    Each person receives an email with a secure link to join your team
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Invitation Details

**Expiration:** Invitations expire after **72 hours (3 days)**

**Email Contains:**
- Who invited them
- Account name
- Secure acceptance link
- Expiration date

## Accepting Invitations

When someone receives an invitation:

1. They click the link in the email
2. **If they don't have an account:** They sign up with the invited email
3. **If they already have an account:** They sign in and accept the invitation
4. They're added to your team as a Member
5. The account appears in their account switcher

## Managing Pending Invitations

View and manage invitations you've sent:

1. Go to **Settings → Team**
2. Click the **Pending Invitations** tab

**You can:**
- **Resend** - Send a new invitation email with a fresh 72-hour expiration
- **Cancel** - Delete the invitation (the link becomes invalid)

<Tip>
If someone doesn't receive the invitation, check their spam folder or resend it.
</Tip>

## Viewing Team Members

The team page shows all members with:
- Name and email
- Status (Active or Pending invitation)
- Last login date
- Join date

**Search and filter:**
- Search by name or email
- Filter by status (Active vs Pending)

## Removing Team Members

<Warning>
Removed members lose access immediately.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Team Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings → Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Member">
    Locate the team member you want to remove
  </Step>

  <Step title="Remove">
    Click the menu (⋯) next to their name → **Remove from Team**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    Confirm the removal

    They can no longer access this account
  </Step>
</Steps>

**What happens:**
- ❌ Member loses access immediately
- ❌ Account disappears from their account switcher
- ✅ Their created content remains (agents, configurations)
- ✅ They can be re-invited if needed

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Onboarding New Members" icon="user-plus">
    **When someone joins:**
    1. Give them a tour of your agents
    2. Share relevant documentation
    3. Set clear expectations for their role
    4. Check in after their first week
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Managing Invitations" icon="envelope">
    **Tips:**
    - Double-check email addresses before sending
    - Follow up if no response after 2 days
    - Resend if invitation expires
    - Cancel invitations sent to wrong addresses
    - Clean up expired invitations monthly
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Offboarding" icon="user-minus">
    **When someone leaves:**
    1. Remove their access immediately
    2. Revoke any API keys they created
    3. Update shared credentials they had access to
    4. Document their work for handoff
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="External Collaborators" icon="user-tie">
    **For contractors or partners:**
    - Consider using a separate subaccount for clients
    - Set calendar reminders to review access
    - Remove access when the engagement ends
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Invitation not received" icon="envelope">
    **Common causes:**
    - Email in spam folder
    - Typo in email address
    - Email blocked by recipient's server

    **Solutions:**
    1. Ask them to check spam
    2. Verify the email address is correct
    3. Resend the invitation
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Invitation expired" icon="clock">
    **Solution:**
    1. Go to **Settings → Team → Pending Invitations**
    2. Find the expired invitation
    3. Click **Resend**
    4. A new 72-hour invitation is sent
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't remove a member" icon="ban">
    **Possible causes:**
    - You don't have permission
    - The member cannot be removed

    **Solution:**
    Contact your account administrator if you need assistance.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Member can't see the account after accepting" icon="eye-slash">
    **Common causes:**
    - User signed in with a different email
    - Account deactivated after invitation

    **Solutions:**
    - Verify they used the invited email address
    - Have them check all accounts in the account switcher
    - Check if the account is still active
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security Tips

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Verify Email Addresses" icon="shield-check">
    Before inviting:
    - Double-check spelling
    - Verify the domain is correct (watch for .co vs .com)
    - Use business emails for company accounts
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Access Reviews" icon="calendar-check">
    Review team members quarterly:
    - Verify each person still needs access
    - Remove inactive users
    - Document the review
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Departure Checklist" icon="list-check">
    When someone leaves:
    - [ ] Remove from all accounts immediately
    - [ ] Revoke any API keys they created
    - [ ] Change shared passwords they knew
    - [ ] Review their recent activity
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="How long are invitations valid?">
    Invitations expire after **72 hours (3 days)**. You can resend expired invitations to generate a new link.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I send a personal message with the invitation?">
    Not currently. Invitations are sent with a standard email template.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How many people can I invite?">
    Team size depends on your subscription plan. Check your current limits in **Settings → Account**.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can someone accept an invitation after it expires?">
    No. Expired invitation links don't work. Resend the invitation to generate a new valid link.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
