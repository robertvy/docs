## Overview

Account settings allow you to customize your account's appearance, manage basic information, and configure account-level preferences.

## Who Can Manage Settings

- **Owner**: ✅ Full access to all settings
- **Admin**: ✅ Full access to all settings (except billing)
- **Member**: ❌ Cannot access settings
- **Viewer**: ❌ Cannot access settings

## Account Information

### Account Name

Change your account's display name:

1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Update the **Account Name** field
3. Click **Save**

The name appears in:
- Account switcher
- Navigation header
- Email notifications
- Shared reports and links

<Tip>
Use descriptive names like "Acme Corp - Sales Team" instead of generic names like "Account 1".
</Tip>

### Account Status

Activate or deactivate your account:

- **Active**: Normal operation, all features available
- **Inactive**: Account disabled, users cannot access

<Warning>
Only Owners can deactivate accounts. Deactivating does not cancel billing - contact support to cancel your subscription.
</Warning>

## Branding Customization

Personalize your account's visual appearance.

### Logo

Upload a square logo for the account switcher and navigation.

**Requirements:**
- Format: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- Size: 512x512px recommended
- Max file size: 2MB
- Transparent background recommended for best results

**To upload:**
1. Go to **Settings → Branding**
2. Click **Upload Logo**
3. Select your image file
4. Crop if needed
5. Click **Save**

### Wide Logo

Upload a horizontal logo for login pages and emails.

**Requirements:**
- Format: PNG, JPG, or SVG
- Aspect ratio: 3:1 to 4:1 recommended
- Max file size: 2MB
- Transparent background recommended

**Used in:**
- Login and signup pages
- Email templates
- PDF reports
- Shared public pages

### Favicon

Upload a custom browser icon.

**Requirements:**
- Format: ICO, PNG, or SVG
- Size: 32x32px or 16x16px
- Max file size: 100KB
- Simple design works best at small sizes

**To upload:**
1. Go to **Settings → Branding**
2. Click **Upload Favicon**
3. Select your favicon file
4. Click **Save**

### Brand Colors

Customize your account's color scheme.

#### Primary Color

Main accent color used throughout the interface:
- Buttons
- Links
- Active states
- Highlights

**To set:**
1. Go to **Settings → Branding**
2. Click the **Primary Color** picker
3. Select a color or enter a hex code
4. Click **Save**

<Tip>
Choose a color with sufficient contrast for accessibility. We recommend colors with at least 4.5:1 contrast ratio against white.
</Tip>

#### Secondary Color

Supporting accent color for:
- Hover states
- Badges
- Secondary actions
- Charts and graphs

**Best practice:**
- Use a complementary or analogous color to your primary
- Ensure it's distinct from primary for clarity
- Test in both light and dark modes

### Preview Changes

Before saving, preview your branding:

1. Click **Preview** in the branding section
2. See how your logos and colors appear in context
3. Adjust as needed
4. Click **Save** when satisfied

## Account Identifiers

### Account UUID

Unique identifier for your account:
- Used in API requests
- Required for support tickets
- Immutable (never changes)

**Example:** `a1b2c3d4-e5f6-7g8h-9i0j-k1l2m3n4o5p6`

**To find:**
1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Copy the UUID from the **Account ID** field

<Note>
The UUID is safe to share with support but treat it as somewhat sensitive - it identifies your account in API requests.
</Note>

### Account Slug

Human-readable URL identifier:
- Used in public URLs
- Unique across all accounts
- Can be changed (subject to availability)

**Example:** `acme-corp-sales`

**To change:**
1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Update the **Account Slug** field
3. Click **Check Availability**
4. If available, click **Save**

<Warning>
Changing your slug breaks existing public links. Update any bookmarks, documentation, or shared URLs.
</Warning>

## Subaccount Management

If you're a parent account, manage subaccounts:

### Viewing Subaccounts

1. Go to **Settings → Subaccounts**
2. See all direct child accounts
3. View:
   - Subaccount name
   - Owner
   - Member count
   - Creation date
   - Status (active/inactive)

### Creating Subaccounts

See [Creating Accounts](/accounts/creating-accounts) for detailed instructions.

### Editing Subaccounts

To update a subaccount's settings:

1. Go to **Settings → Subaccounts**
2. Click on a subaccount name
3. Or click the menu (⋯) → **Switch To**
4. Update settings in the subaccount's context

### Subaccount Access

As a parent account, you can:
- ✅ View all subaccount data
- ✅ Manage subaccount settings
- ✅ Add/remove subaccount members
- ✅ Access subaccount agents and conversations

## Notification Preferences

Configure how you receive notifications.

### Email Notifications

Control which emails you receive:

- **Account activity**: Member joins, role changes
- **Billing**: Payment receipts, failed charges, plan changes
- **Usage alerts**: Approaching limits, quota warnings
- **Product updates**: New features, platform updates
- **Marketing**: Tips, webinars, promotions (opt-in)

**To configure:**
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Toggle each notification type
3. Changes save automatically

### In-App Notifications

Configure browser notifications:

- **Conversation alerts**: New messages, agent errors
- **Team activity**: Mentions, invitations
- **System alerts**: Maintenance, outages

**To enable:**
1. Go to **Settings → Notifications**
2. Enable **Browser Notifications**
3. Click **Allow** in browser prompt

## Timezone

Set your account's default timezone:

**Used for:**
- Scheduled reports
- Usage graphs
- Conversation timestamps
- Booking calendar availability

**To change:**
1. Go to **Settings → Account**
2. Select **Timezone** from dropdown
3. Click **Save**

<Note>
Individual users can override this with their personal timezone preference in their profile settings.
</Note>

## Data & Privacy

### Data Retention

Configure how long conversation data is retained:

| Plan | Retention Period |
|------|------------------|
| Free | 7 days |
| Starter | 30 days |
| Pro | 90 days |
| Enterprise | Custom (up to 2 years) |

**To request changes:**
Contact support to adjust retention settings for compliance requirements.

### Data Export

Export your account data:

1. Go to **Settings → Data & Privacy**
2. Click **Request Data Export**
3. Select data types:
   - Agents and configurations
   - Conversation transcripts
   - Team members
   - Knowledge bases
4. Click **Create Export**
5. Receive download link via email (within 24 hours)

**Export format:** JSON or CSV

### Account Deletion

To permanently delete your account:

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. All data, including agents, conversations, and team members, will be permanently deleted.
</Warning>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Backup Your Data">
    Request a data export first to backup important information
  </Step>

  <Step title="Cancel Subscription">
    Cancel your subscription and settle any outstanding invoices
  </Step>

  <Step title="Delete Subaccounts">
    Delete or transfer all subaccounts first
  </Step>

  <Step title="Request Deletion">
    Contact support at support@itellico.ai to request account deletion
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Deletion">
    Support will verify and process your request within 3-5 business days
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Logo not appearing" icon="image">
    **Possible causes:**
    - File size too large
    - Unsupported format
    - Browser cache

    **Solutions:**
    - Compress image to under 2MB
    - Convert to PNG or JPG
    - Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Colors not updating" icon="palette">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Not saved after changing
    - Browser cache
    - Custom CSS overriding

    **Solutions:**
    - Click Save after color changes
    - Clear browser cache
    - Check for custom stylesheets
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can't change slug" icon="link">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Slug already taken
    - Invalid characters
    - Too short/long

    **Solutions:**
    - Try a different slug
    - Use only letters, numbers, hyphens
    - Must be 3-50 characters
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Settings grayed out" icon="lock">
    **Possible causes:**
    - Not Owner or Admin
    - Account inactive
    - Feature not in your plan

    **Solutions:**
    - Ask Owner to change settings
    - Upgrade plan if needed
    - Contact support
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
