## Overview

itellicoAI uses a role-based access control system with four distinct roles. Each role has specific permissions designed for different team responsibilities.

## The Four Roles

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Owner" icon="crown">
    Full administrative control of the account
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin" icon="user-shield">
    Administrative privileges without billing access

  <Card title="Member" icon="user">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Viewer" icon="eye">

  </Card>

## Owner

**Full administrative control** of the account.

### Permissions

✅ **All permissions** (wildcard access)
- Manage team members and roles
- Manage account settings and branding
- Create and manage subaccounts
- Manage billing and subscriptions
- **Delete the account**
- Cannot be removed by others

### Restrictions

- Only **one Owner** per account
- Owner role cannot be transferred via UI (contact support)
- Cannot be removed by other team members

### Best For

- Company leadership
- Account administrators
- Primary points of contact

<Warning>
The Owner role has unrestricted access, including the ability to delete the account. Choose carefully!
</Warning>

---

## Admin

**Administrative privileges** without billing access.

### Permissions

✅ **Team Management**
- Add, remove, and manage team members
- Change member roles (except Owner)
- Send and manage invitations

✅ **Account Management**
- Manage account settings
- Create and manage subaccounts (if subscription allows)
- Manage integrations and API keys

✅ **Full Feature Access**
- Create and edit agents
- Access all conversations
- Manage knowledge bases
- Configure actions and tools

### Restrictions

❌ **Cannot:**
- Delete the account
- Remove the Owner
- Access billing settings
- Transfer ownership

### Best For

- Technical leads
- Team managers
- Senior developers
- Client account managers

---

## Member

**Standard user** with full access to features.

### Permissions

✅ **Content Creation**
- Create and edit agents
- Manage own agents and configurations
- Use test features
- Create and manage conversations

✅ **Content Access**
- Access all agents in the account
- View all conversation logs
- View and use knowledge bases
- View team members

### Restrictions

❌ **Cannot:**
- Manage team members
- Change account settings
- Create subaccounts
- Manage billing
- Create API keys
- Remove other members

### Best For

- Developers
- Content creators
- Customer support staff
- Most team members

---

## Viewer

**Read-only access** to the account.

### Permissions

✅ **View Only**
- View agents and configurations
- View conversation logs and transcripts
- View team members
- View analytics and reports

### Restrictions

❌ **Cannot:**
- Create or edit anything
- Access settings
- Invite members
- Make any changes to the account

### Best For

- Stakeholders
- Auditors
- Clients who need visibility
- Reporting staff

## Choosing the Right Role

### Decision Tree

```
Need to manage billing?
├─ Yes → Owner
└─ No → Need to manage team members?
    ├─ Yes → Admin
    └─ No → Need to create/edit content?
        ├─ Yes → Member
        └─ No → Viewer
```

### Common Role Assignments

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Small Team (2-5 people)" icon="users">
    - **1 Owner**: Founder or CEO
    - **1-2 Admins**: Technical leads or managers
    - **1-2 Members**: Developers or creators
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Agency" icon="briefcase">
    - **1 Owner**: Agency owner or operations lead
    - **2-3 Admins**: Client success managers
    - **5-10 Members**: Content creators and developers
    - **Viewers**: Optional for stakeholders
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Enterprise" icon="building">
    - **1 Owner**: IT director or CTO
    - **5-10 Admins**: Department heads and team leads
    - **20-100 Members**: Staff across departments
    - **10-20 Viewers**: Executive leadership, compliance
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Client Subaccounts (Agency)" icon="user-tie">
    - **1 Owner**: Client primary contact
    - **0-2 Admins**: Client team leads
    - **1-5 Members**: Client staff
    - **0-3 Viewers**: Client stakeholders
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Changing Roles

### Who Can Change Roles

- **Owners**: Can change any role (except Owner)
- **Admins**: Can change Member and Viewer roles
- **Members**: Cannot change roles
- **Viewers**: Cannot change roles

### How to Change a Member's Role

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Team Settings">
    Go to **Settings → Team** in your account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Find the Member">
    Locate the team member in the list
  </Step>

  <Step title="Change Role">
    Click the role dropdown next to their name and select the new role
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm">
    The role change takes effect immediately
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
Role changes are immediate and permanent. The user's permissions update instantly.
</Warning>

## Special Scenarios

### Transferring Ownership

To transfer the Owner role:

1. Contact support at support@itellico.ai
2. Provide:
   - Current owner email
   - New owner email
   - Account name and UUID
   - Confirmation from current owner
3. Support will verify and process the transfer

<Note>
Ownership transfers cannot be done through the UI for security reasons. This prevents accidental or unauthorized transfers.
</Note>

### Multiple Owners

**Not Supported**: Each account can have only one Owner.

**Why?**
- Clear accountability for billing
- Prevents conflicting administrative decisions
- Simplifies ownership transfer process

**Alternative**: Promote additional users to Admin role for nearly equivalent access (without billing control).

### External Consultants

For temporary team members like consultants:

**Best Practice:**
- Assign **Viewer** role initially
- Promote to **Member** if they need to create content
- **Never** give Admin or Owner to external parties
- Remove access when engagement ends

## Security Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Principle of Least Privilege" icon="shield-halved">
    Always assign the minimum role needed:

    - Need to observe? → Viewer
    - Need to create? → Member
    - Need to manage team? → Admin
    - Need billing access? → Owner
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Regular Audits" icon="clipboard-list">
    Review team members quarterly:

    - Remove inactive users
    - Demote users who changed responsibilities
    - Verify external consultants still need access
    - Check if any Admins should be Members
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Protect Owner Access" icon="crown">
    - Limit Owner role to 1-2 trusted individuals
    - Use strong passwords and 2FA
    - Never share Owner credentials
    - Plan ownership succession
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Document Role Assignments" icon="file-lines">
    Maintain a record of:

    - Why each person has their role
    - When they were granted access
    - When access should be reviewed
    - Approval for Admin/Owner roles
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I have multiple Owners?">
    No. Each account can have only one Owner for clear accountability.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if the Owner leaves?">
    Contact support to transfer ownership to another team member. Provide verification and they'll process the transfer.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Admins see billing information?">
    No. Only the Owner can access billing and subscription details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I remove myself from an account?">
    - **Owner**: No, you must transfer ownership first
    - **Admin/Member/Viewer**: Yes, you can remove yourself
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do role changes apply to subaccounts?">
    No. Roles are per-account. A user might be an Owner in a subaccount but a Member in the parent.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can Viewers export data?">
    No. Viewers have read-only access and cannot export or download data.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
