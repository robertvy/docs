## January 2026

### New Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Smart Filler" icon="bolt">
    **Reduce perceived latency with intelligent filler responses**

    Smart Filler uses a fast model to generate contextual acknowledgments ("Let me check that for you...") while your main LLM processes the full response. Reduces perceived latency by ~500ms.

    - Customizable filler prompts for brand voice consistency
    - Language-aware filler generation
    - Works alongside Thinking Sounds for comprehensive latency management

    [Learn more →](/build/advanced/smart-filler)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Thinking Sounds" icon="keyboard">
    **Audio feedback during agent processing**

    Add realistic keyboard typing sounds while your agent processes responses. Creates a natural "someone is working on your request" feeling during function calls and LLM processing.

    - Automatic triggering during processing states
    - Configurable in Voice > Sound Effects
    - Complements Smart Filler for natural conversation flow

    [Learn more →](/build/voice-speech/thinking-sounds)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Web Search Action" icon="magnifying-glass">
    **Real-time web search during conversations**

    Enable your agent to search the web for current information using Tavily-powered search. Perfect for questions about recent events, competitor information, or data not in your knowledge base.

    - Configurable search depth (Basic/Advanced)
    - Domain restrictions for focused searches
    - AI-generated answer summaries
    - Customizable filler phrases during search

    [Learn more →](/build/actions/web-search)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Voice Cloning" icon="clone">
    **Create custom voices from audio samples**

    Upload voice samples to create custom AI voices for your agents. Perfect for brand consistency or unique character voices.

    - Support for MP3, WAV, M4A, OGG formats
    - Organization-wide voice library
    - Works with all voice settings and pronunciations

    [Learn more →](/build/voice-speech/voice-cloning)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Recording Opt-Out" icon="shield-halved">
    **Allow callers to stop and delete recordings**

    Enable GDPR-compliant recording opt-out. When activated, callers can request to stop recording and delete any recorded audio during the call.

    - Automatic recognition of opt-out requests
    - Immediate recording stop and deletion
    - Configurable per agent

    [Learn more →](/build/advanced/recording-opt-out)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Widget Trust Center" icon="building-shield">
    **Public privacy information for web widgets**

    Create a public Trust Center page for your web widgets. Show visitors your privacy practices, link to your privacy policy, and list data subprocessors.

    - Auto-generated public URL
    - Customizable privacy policy and subprocessors links
    - Builds customer trust before conversations

    [Learn more →](/launch/web-widget-deployment#widget-trust-center)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Custom MCP Servers" icon="server">
    **Connect your own MCP servers**

    Full support for custom Model Context Protocol servers. Connect your own MCP endpoints with custom headers, query parameters, and tool management.

    - Direct URL connection (Retell-style)
    - Automatic tool discovery
    - Granular tool enable/disable
    - Configurable timeouts

    [Learn more →](/build/advanced/mcp-servers)
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

### Improvements

- **Announcements:** Enhanced audio upload support with MP3, OGG, WAV formats (up to 10MB)
- **Custom Ambient Sounds:** Upload your own background audio for unique agent atmospheres
- **Widget Share Links:** Create demo links with expiration dates and usage limits
- **Performance:** Improved response times across all voice providers
