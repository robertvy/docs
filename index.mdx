> Welcome to itellicoAI - your solution for automating phone and web conversations with intelligent AI agents.

## What is itellicoAI?

itellicoAI helps businesses and developers automate customer conversations using AI voice technology. From handling phone calls to powering web-based voice interactions, our platform manages the technical complexity while you focus on delivering exceptional customer experiences.

**With itellicoAI, you can:**
- Automate inbound and outbound phone conversations
- Handle customer inquiries 24/7 without human intervention
- Connect voice agents to your business systems via APIs
- Deploy voice interactions on your website
- Streamline workflows like scheduling, support, and sales

## Understanding AI Voice Technology

itellicoAI agents work by orchestrating three essential components:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Transcription" icon="microphone">

  </Card>
  <Card title="AI Processing" icon="brain">
    Understands context and determines appropriate responses using advanced language models

  <Card title="Voice Synthesis" icon="volume-high">
    Generates natural-sounding speech from text responses
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

Our platform supports multiple AI providers including OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, Deepgram, and ElevenLabs, giving you flexibility to choose the best fit for your needs.
