## Overview

The Booking action transforms your AI agents into powerful scheduling assistants that can book appointments during live conversations. Integrated with Cal.com, your agents can check real availability, present time slots, confirm bookings, and send automatic notifications - all without handing the customer off to a human or separate booking system.

Imagine calling a business and having your appointment scheduled, confirmed, and added to your calendar in a single conversation. That's the power of the Booking action.

<Note>
Booking actions require a Cal.com account and active integration. The integration is free to set up and works with Cal.com's free tier, though premium Cal.com features require appropriate Cal.com subscription.
</Note>

## Before You Start

### Prerequisites

<Steps>
  <Step title="Cal.com Account">
    Create a Cal.com account at [cal.com](https://cal.com) if you don't have one
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Integration">
    Go to **Integrations → Cal.com** in your itellicoAI dashboard and connect your Cal.com account

    The booking action will remain hidden until this integration status shows "Connected"
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Event Types">
    Set up Cal.com event types for each type of appointment your agent should book:
    - Define duration (15 min, 30 min, 60 min, etc.)
    - Configure hosts/assignees
    - Set meeting location (Zoom, Google Meet, phone, in-person)
    - Define booking windows and availability
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Meeting Platforms">
    In Cal.com, connect your meeting platforms:
    - Zoom account
    - Google Meet
    - Microsoft Teams
    - Phone
    - Custom links

    Each event type can use a different platform
  </Step>
  <Step title="Plan Notification Strategy">
    Decide notification approach:
    - Cal.com sends email confirmations automatically
    - Optionally add SMS confirmations from itellicoAI
    - Configure reminder settings in Cal.com
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Test your Cal.com event types manually first by booking test appointments. This ensures availability windows, meeting links, and notifications work correctly before connecting to your AI agent.
</Tip>

## Cal.com Integration Setup

### Connecting Cal.com to itellicoAI

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    In your itellicoAI dashboard, go to **Integrations → Cal.com**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Connect">
    Click **Connect Cal.com** button to initiate OAuth flow
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    Log into Cal.com and authorize itellicoAI to:
    - Read your event types
    - Check availability
    - Create bookings on your behalf
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Connection">
    Return to itellicoAI and verify status shows "Connected"

    You should see your Cal.com username and connection timestamp
  </Step>
  <Step title="Disconnect (if needed)">
    To disconnect, click **Disconnect** - this revokes itellicoAI's access to Cal.com
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Adding a Booking Action

Once Cal.com is connected, you can add booking actions to your agents:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Agent Editor">
    Navigate to your agent and click **Abilities → Actions** tab
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Booking Action">
    Click **Add Action** and select **Calendar Booking**

    If Cal.com isn't connected, you'll be redirected to complete integration first
  </Step>
  <Step title="Name Your Action">
    Give it a clear name like "Book Consultation" or "Schedule Demo"

    This exact name will be referenced in your agent's instructions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Cal.com Event">
    Complete the event configuration (detailed below)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Email Handling">
    Set up how email addresses are collected and used (detailed below)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Scheduling Preferences">
    Set timezone, date ranges, and slot preferences (detailed below)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Optional: Add SMS Notifications">
    Configure SMS confirmations if desired (detailed below)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Action">
    Click **Save** to make the booking action available to your agent
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Email Handling

Email addresses are required for booking confirmations. The platform uses a priority system to determine which email to use:

### Email Priority Order

The booking action checks for email addresses in this order:

1. **Context endpoint data** - Email returned by your custom context API (field: `cal_email`)
2. **Contact record** - Email stored on the itellicoAI contact
3. **Fallback email** - Team inbox configured in action settings
4. **Ask customer** - Agent politely requests email during conversation

<Note>
If no email is found through steps 1-3, your agent will ask the customer for their email address before completing the booking.
</Note>

### Fallback Email Configuration

Configure a fallback email for situations where customer email is unknown:

**Use cases:**
- Anonymous callers
- Customers without email in system
- General inquiries where email isn't required upfront
- Group bookings where organizer books on behalf of others

**Configuration:**
```
┌─ Email Handling ─────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                               │
│ Email Priority Order:                                         │
│ 1. Context API (cal_email field)                             │
│ 2. Contact record (contact.email)                            │
│ 3. Fallback email (below)                                    │
│ 4. Ask customer during call                                  │
│                                                               │
│ Fallback Email (Optional)                                    │
│ [bookings@company.com_____________________]                  │
│                                                               │
│ ℹ️  Booking will be created under this email if customer's  │
│    email is not available                                    │
│                                                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

<Warning>
Using a fallback email means bookings may not be properly attributed to the actual customer. Use for convenience, but encourage agents to collect real email addresses when possible.
</Warning>

### Asking for Email During Conversation

When the agent needs to collect email:

**Good prompting:**
```
Instructions for collecting email:

If you need to book an appointment and don't have the customer's email:
1. Explain why: "To send your confirmation and meeting details, I'll need your email address"
2. Ask clearly: "What email address should I use?"
3. Confirm: "Great, I'll send confirmation to [repeat email]"
4. If they don't have email, offer fallback: "No problem, I can still book this for you"
```

## SMS Notifications (Optional)

Enhance your booking experience with automatic SMS confirmations:

### Enabling SMS Confirmations

<Steps>
  <Step title="Toggle SMS">
    Enable "Send SMS confirmation" in booking action configuration
  </Step>

  </Accordion>

## Scheduling Preferences

Fine-tune how your agent presents and books time slots:

### Default Booking Instructions

**Toggle: "Use default booking instructions"**
- **Enabled (default):** Platform provides pre-built scheduling script for the agent
- **Disabled:** You handle all scheduling logic manually in your instructions

Most users should leave this enabled. Disable only if you need complete custom control over the booking flow.

### Timezone Configuration

Set the timezone for suggested time slots:

**Options:**
- **Specific timezone:** Anchor suggestions to a timezone (e.g., America/New_York)
- **Blank/Default:** Use Cal.com event type's default timezone
- **Customer timezone:** Use contact's timezone if available

```
┌─ Scheduling Preferences ─────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                               │
│ ☑ Use default booking instructions                           │
│                                                               │
│ Timezone                                    [Dropdown ▼]     │
│ [America/New_York_____________________________]              │
│                                                               │
│ Common timezones:                                             │
│ - America/New_York (EST/EDT)                                 │
│ - America/Chicago (CST/CDT)                                  │
│ - America/Los_Angeles (PST/PDT)                              │
│ - Europe/London (GMT/BST)                                    │
│ - UTC                                                        │
│                                                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

<Tip>
If your business operates in one timezone and most customers are local, set a specific timezone. If you serve customers globally, leave blank and let Cal.com handle timezone conversion.
</Tip>

### Scheduling Windows

Control which dates your agent can offer:

**Start Date:**
- Earliest date the agent can suggest
- Useful for launches, seasonal availability, or advance booking minimums
- Leave blank for immediate availability

**Days to Look Ahead (1-3):**
- How many future days the agent considers when finding slots
- **1 day:** Today only (or tomorrow if outside hours)
- **2 days:** Today and tomorrow
- **3 days:** Today and next 2 days

```
┌─ Scheduling Window ──────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                               │
│ Start Date (Optional)                                         │
│ [2025-02-01_______________________] 📅                       │
│                                                               │
│ Days to look ahead*                                           │
│ ├───┼───┼───┤                                                │
│ 1   2   3                                                    │
│     ●                                                         │
│                                                               │
│ Agent will suggest slots from the next 2 days                │
│                                                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Slot Presentation Settings

Control how many options the agent presents:

**Time Slots Per Day (2-5):**
- How many time slots per day the agent offers
- Higher numbers give more choices
- Lower numbers simplify decision-making

**Hours Between Suggestions (1-4):**
- Minimum spacing between suggested times
- Prevents overwhelming customer with back-to-back options
- Creates variety in presentation

```
┌─ Slot Presentation ──────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                               │
│ Time slots per day*                                           │
│ ├───┼───┼───┼───┼───┤                                        │
│ 2   3   4   5                                                │
│         ●                                                     │
│                                                               │
│ Hours between suggestions*                                    │
│ ├───┼───┼───┼───┤                                            │
│ 1   2   3   4                                                │
│     ●                                                         │
│                                                               │
│ Example: 10am, 12pm, 2pm, 4pm (3 slots, 2 hours apart)      │
│                                                               │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

### Recommended Settings by Use Case

**High-volume appointment setting:**
- Days ahead: 1-2
- Slots per day: 4-5
- Hours between: 1-2
- Goal: Fill calendar quickly with many options

**Executive scheduling:**
- Days ahead: 3
- Slots per day: 2-3
- Hours between: 3-4
- Goal: Curated, spacious options

**Same-day booking urgency:**
- Days ahead: 1
- Slots per day: 5
- Hours between: 1
- Goal: Maximize today's availability

**Considered purchase consultation:**
- Days ahead: 3
- Slots per day: 3
- Hours between: 2-3
- Goal: Thoughtful scheduling with choice

## Using Booking Actions in Instructions

### Basic Booking Flow

Reference the booking action by exact name in your instructions:

```
When a customer wants to schedule a consultation:

1. Confirm they want to book: "I'd be happy to schedule that for you"

2. Ask about their availability:
   "What days work best for you this week?"

3. If email not in contact record, collect it:
   "What email should I use for the confirmation?"

4. Use the 'Book Consultation' action to present available times

5. Once confirmed, verbally summarize:
   "Perfect! You're all set for [day] at [time]. You'll receive
   a confirmation email with the Zoom link."

6. Ask if anything else needed, then end call politely
```

### Collecting Required Information

Ensure the agent gathers necessary data before booking:

```
Before using 'Book Consultation' action:

Required information:
✅ Customer wants to book (not just asking about availability)
✅ Customer's email address (if not in system)
✅ General timeframe preference (this week, next week, mornings, etc.)

Optional but helpful:
- Specific reason for meeting
- Any special requirements
- Preferred meeting platform (if you offer options)

Don't attempt booking without email address.
```

### Handling Booking Failures

Instruct your agent what to do if bookings fail:

```
If the 'Book Consultation' action fails:

Common reasons:
- No availability in the requested timeframe
- Cal.com service temporarily unavailable
- Network connectivity issue

Response:
1. Apologize sincerely: "I apologize, I'm having trouble accessing
   the calendar right now"

2. Offer alternative:
   - "I can have someone from our team call you back to schedule"
   - Collect callback number and best time
   - OR transfer to scheduling team if available

3. If failure is "no availability":
   - "Unfortunately, we're fully booked for [timeframe]"
   - "I can check [alternative timeframe]"
   - Or: "Would you like me to add you to our waitlist?"
```

### Multiple Booking Actions

If you have multiple event types, guide the agent on which to use:

```
Booking Actions Available:
- 'Book 15-Min Quick Call' - For brief questions, account reviews
- 'Book 30-Min Discovery Call' - For initial consultations, demos
- 'Book 60-Min Deep Dive' - For detailed planning, implementations

Selection criteria:

Quick Call (15 min):
- Simple questions
- Account status checks
- Brief follow-ups

Discovery Call (30 min):
- Product demos
- Initial consultations
- Qualification calls

Deep Dive (60 min):
- Implementation planning
- Training sessions
- Strategic reviews

If unsure, ask customer: "Would a 30-minute discovery call work,
or would you prefer a longer session?"
```

## Reminders & Follow-Up

### Cal.com Reminder Configuration

Cal.com handles reminder emails automatically:

**Configure in Cal.com:**
1. Go to event type settings in Cal.com
2. Navigate to "Email Notifications"
3. Set up reminders:
   - 24 hours before
   - 1 hour before
   - Custom timing

**Email reminders can include:**
- Meeting details (date, time, duration)
- Meeting link (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.)
- Reschedule/cancel links
- Preparation instructions
- Custom messages

<Note>
Reminder configuration happens in Cal.com, not itellicoAI. Changes to reminder timing require updating the Cal.com event type settings.
</Note>

### Fallback Email for Reminders

If using a fallback email address:

**Reminders will go to:**
- The fallback email (your team inbox)
- NOT the actual customer (since their email wasn't used)

**Workarounds:**
1. Always collect real customer email when possible
2. Use SMS confirmations to reach customer directly
3. Have team forward reminders manually
4. Build custom reminder system via webhooks

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Booking Action Not Appearing" icon="eye-slash">
    **Symptom:** Can't find Calendar Booking in action list

    **Cause:** Cal.com integration not connected

    **Solution:**
    - Go to Integrations → Cal.com
    - Click "Connect Cal.com"
    - Complete OAuth authorization
    - Return to agent and retry adding action
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="No Event Types in Dropdown" icon="calendar-xmark">
    **Symptom:** Event type dropdown is empty

    **Causes:**
    - No event types created in Cal.com
    - Event types not synced yet
    - Integration connection issue

    **Solutions:**
    - Create event types in Cal.com first
    - Click "Refresh" in Cal.com integration settings
    - Disconnect and reconnect integration
    - Wait 30 seconds and retry
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Meeting Platform Disabled" icon="video-slash">
    **Symptom:** "Configure in Cal.com" shown for meeting platform

    **Cause:** Platform not enabled for this event type

    **Solution:**
    - Go to Cal.com event type settings
    - Click "Add location"
    - Enable desired platform (Zoom, Google Meet, etc.)
    - Connect platform account if needed
    - Return to itellicoAI and refresh
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Booking Fails with 'No Availability'" icon="calendar-slash">
    **Symptom:** Agent reports no available times

    **Causes:**
    - Availability windows too restrictive
    - All slots already booked
    - Date range outside scheduling window
    - Timezone misconfiguration

    **Solutions:**
    - Check Cal.com availability settings
    - Review existing bookings
    - Adjust "days to look ahead" setting
    - Verify timezone configuration
    - Test booking manually in Cal.com
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Email Confirmation Not Received" icon="envelope-slash">
    **Symptom:** Booking succeeds but no email arrives

    **Causes:**
    - Email in spam folder
    - Cal.com email notifications disabled
    - Wrong email address used
    - Email service delay

    **Solutions:**
    - Check spam/junk folders
    - Verify email notifications enabled in Cal.com event settings
    - Check which email was used for booking
    - Wait 5-10 minutes for delivery
    - Check Cal.com activity log
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="SMS Confirmation Not Sending" icon="message-slash">
    **Symptom:** Email arrives but SMS doesn't

    **Causes:**
    - SMS toggle not enabled
    - No phone number in contact record
    - Invalid phone number format
    - SMS sending error

    **Solutions:**
    - Verify "Send SMS confirmation" is checked
    - Check contact has valid phone number
    - Ensure number in E.164 format (+14155551234)
    - Review SMS template for errors
    - Check account SMS balance/settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Meeting Link Not Working" icon="link-slash">
    **Symptom:** Zoom/Meet link doesn't connect

    **Causes:**
    - Meeting platform not properly connected to Cal.com
    - Link expired (for instant meetings)
    - Platform account issue

    **Solutions:**
    - In Cal.com, disconnect and reconnect meeting platform
    - Create test event in Cal.com to verify platform works
    - Check Zoom/Google account status
    - Try different meeting platform
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong Timezone for Appointments" icon="clock">
    **Symptom:** Times are off by several hours

    **Causes:**
    - Timezone setting mismatch
    - Customer timezone not considered
    - Cal.com default timezone wrong

    **Solutions:**
    - Check timezone setting in booking action
    - Verify Cal.com event type timezone
    - Use customer's timezone if available
    - Always confirm time verbally with customer
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Agent Doesn't Use Booking Action" icon="robot">
    **Symptom:** Agent doesn't attempt to book appointments

    **Causes:**
    - Action name not referenced in instructions
    - Instructions unclear about when to book
    - Agent doesn't recognize booking intent

    **Solutions:**
    - Add explicit instruction: "Use the 'Book Consultation' action"
    - Reference action by exact name
    - Add examples of booking scenarios
    - Test with explicit request: "Book me an appointment"
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Confirm Details Verbally" icon="microphone">
    After booking, always have the agent verbally confirm the appointment:

    ```
    After successful booking:
    "Perfect! I've scheduled your [event type] for [day of week],
    [month] [date] at [time] [timezone]. You'll receive a
    confirmation email at [email] with the [Zoom/Meet] link.
    The meeting will last [duration]."
    ```

    This prevents miscommunication and builds confidence.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Collect Email Early" icon="envelope">
    Get email addresses before checking availability:

    ```
    Booking conversation flow:
    1. Confirm customer wants to book
    2. Collect email: "What email should I send the confirmation to?"
    3. Ask about preferred dates/times
    4. Use booking action to present options
    5. Confirm selection
    ```

    This prevents having to ask for email after finding perfect time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Set Clear Expectations" icon="bullseye">
    Tell customers what will happen:

    ```
    "I'll check our calendar for available times this week,
    then send you a confirmation email with the Zoom link.
    You'll also get a reminder 24 hours before the meeting."
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Handle 'No Availability' Gracefully" icon="calendar-xmark">
    Have a plan when no slots are available:

    ```
    If no availability in requested timeframe:
    1. Apologize: "I'm sorry, we're fully booked for [timeframe]"
    2. Offer alternatives:
       - Check different dates: "I have openings next week"
       - Offer waitlist: "Would you like me to add you to our waitlist?"
       - Transfer: "Let me connect you with scheduling to find a time"
    ```
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Use Appropriate Event Types" icon="calendar-check">
    Create distinct event types for different purposes:

    - 15-min Quick Calls
    - 30-min Consultations
    - 60-min Deep Dives
    - In-Person Meetings

    Give agents clear guidance on which to use when.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Test Regularly" icon="vial">
    Schedule regular test bookings to ensure everything works:
    - Weekly test bookings
    - After Cal.com configuration changes
    - Before high-volume campaigns
    - When adding new event types
    - After platform updates
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Monitor Booking Success Rate" icon="chart-line">
    Track how often bookings succeed vs. fail:
    - Review conversation transcripts
    - Check Cal.com booking completion rate
    - Identify common failure patterns
    - Adjust instructions based on data
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
